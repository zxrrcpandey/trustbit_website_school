Founded in 1995, Trustbit Books & Stationery began as a small neighborhood shop with a simple mission: to make quality educational supplies accessible to every family.

Over the years, we've grown from a single store to become one of the region's most trusted names in school supplies. What started as a passion project has blossomed into a comprehensive educational resource center, serving thousands of students and families every year.

Our founder, Mr. Kishore Gupta, believed that every child deserves access to quality educational materials. This belief continues to guide us today as we work tirelessly to bring you the best products at the most competitive prices.
